What MuseR does

Three ways to use a MuseR studio

Home studio owners list their space, instruments, and skills. Musicians book what they need, by the hour, safely.

🏠

Book a space

Acoustically treated home studios across London. Verified photos, house rules, and a calendar-based booking system. Pay only for the hours you use.

🎸

Rent gear & software

Instruments, amps, DAWs, and plugin libraries available by the hour. Professional tools without the professional price tag.

🎛️

Record with an engineer

Host-assisted recording sessions with local producers at transparent, agreed rates. Both parties verified. Both parties protected.

"Making music costs more than most musicians earn. MuseR is built to change that — not by disrupting the industry, but by unlocking what's already there."

Platform principles

  • Every host is manually verified before any listing goes live
  • Every guest is ID-verified before they can browse listings
  • Payments are held in escrow until the session is complete
  • The full address is only revealed after a booking is confirmed
  • Blind mutual reviews keep ratings honest for both sides
  • MuseR never touches, owns, or monetises the music itself

About MuseR

Built from inside
the community

MuseR was founded by someone who lives in London's independent music scene — not parachuted in from outside it. The problem is real. The supply exists. The gap is uncontested. We are building the platform to bridge it.

🎯 The mission

Remove the single biggest barrier between raw talent and a finished recording — cost. MuseR is not a luxury product. It is an access product, designed from the ground up for independent artists who are serious about their music but self-funding their career.

🔐 The trust model

Both sides of this marketplace are taking a risk. Guests enter a stranger's home. Hosts share expensive equipment. Every feature on MuseR — from mandatory ID verification to encrypted address storage — exists because we take that risk seriously for both parties.

📍 Starting in London

London is the UK's dominant music hub — and the ideal proof-of-concept city. We are beginning with a hyper-local invite-only beta in East and South London, where the density of home studios and independent artists is highest.

🌍 The long-term vision

MuseR starts as a studio booking platform and ends as the full infrastructure layer for independent music — globally. Artist profiles, collaboration tools, a talent marketplace. Everywhere there are musicians who need a space to create.
